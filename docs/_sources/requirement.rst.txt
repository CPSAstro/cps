
Requirements
============

The cpsAStro package requires the following packages:

Python 3.6 or later

`Numpy 1.19.4 <https://numpy.org/devdocs/release/1.16.0-notes.html>`_ or later

`Astropy 4.1  <https://www.astropy.org/announcements/release-2.0.html>`_  or later

`astrquery 0.4.1 <https://astroquery.readthedocs.io/en/latest/>`_  or later

`photutils 1.0.1 <https://photutils.readthedocs.io/en/stable/>`_  or later

`requests 2.24.0 <https://pypi.org/project/requests/>`_  or later
